    • Digital Art

    I started my journey of digital art in 2018 when I first got my drawing tablet. I always admired art that is authentic and original, so when it came to expressing my own self, weirdness or rules did not hold me back. I am eager to learn more and discover new things, but authenticity to self will always guide me.

    Creating for You

    On my journey I had opportunities in creating art for other people as well. It brings me warmth being part of something bigger than myself and working on projects that bring other people joy.
    So here I am.
    Ready to create for you too.

    Creating for streamers.

    I learned to enjoy stepping out of my comfort zone when it comes to creating art and make something completely out of my style in order to fit the user needs. This way I've created twitch emotes and profile illustrations for amazing creators.

    Creating for a small serious game developer company Cloudwhale.

    I received some great experience while working for this company. Spending my time on trying to make other peoples ideas come to life helped me understand other people better and the art process behind creating such art.

    Game Development

    "Leaves"

    In 2021 I had a chance to create my own game. Through that project I discovered how much I really love making games and how magical it is to finally see it take life.

    The project I worked on I called "Leaves". It was inspired by a book "House of Leaves" by Mark Z. Danielewski.

    I was able to create a small demo for this game completely from scratch
    and I am really passionate about it.

    "Greenhouse"

    In 2023 I worked on a serious game meant for people with general anxiety disorder or its symptoms.

    I called this project "Greenhouse". After thorough research into the disorder as well as different treatments and therapies that are currently applied, I have created a demo for a soothing and relaxing game that would try to help such people.

    The process of working on the project has been a mind opening experience for me, since it showed how many different ways we can help people through games.

    It strengthened my wish to make an impact through such ways.
